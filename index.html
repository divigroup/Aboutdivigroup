Track Shared Expenses with Ease

Splitzy helps friends, roommates, and families split group expenses fairly and settle balances effortlessly. Whether you're on a trip or managing monthly bills, Splitzy keeps everyone informed and organized.

Key Features

  • Chat-style expense tracker for easy input
  • Create groups and split bills among members
  • Settle balances with integrated UPI support
  • Track how much you owe or are owed
  • Push notifications for important actions
  • Data secured and synced using Firebase Auth + MongoDB

Why We Request Your Data

We use your email and basic profile information to personalize your experience, manage group memberships, and send important notifications. Your data is never sold and only used to provide core functionality within the app.
