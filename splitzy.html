What is Splitzy?

Splitzy is an app that helps you track and split expenses with friends, roommates, or teammates. Whether you're planning a trip or managing shared living costs, Splitzy ensures fair and easy settlement.

  • Create & manage expense groups
  • Track contributions and debts
  • Send reminders and settle balances
  • Push notifications & UPI-ready

Why We Request Data

We ask for your name and email address to:

  • Securely authenticate your account
  • Associate you with expense groups
  • Send balance reminders & alerts
Your data is never sold or shared without consent.
